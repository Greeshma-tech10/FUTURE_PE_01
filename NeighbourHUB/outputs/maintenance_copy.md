# Society Maintenance & Management

## Simplify Everyday Society Operations

Manage maintenance workflows, resident requests, payments, and community facilities from one centralized platform.

NeighbourHUB helps apartment associations and management committees operate more efficiently while delivering a smoother living experience for residents.

---

## Features

### Maintenance Request Management
Allow residents to raise maintenance issues instantly and track progress in real time.

### Complaint Tracking
Manage resident complaints with organized workflows, updates, and faster resolution times.

### Society Payments
Collect maintenance dues, utility payments, and other society charges securely through the platform.

### Notices & Announcements
Share important updates, circulars, and community notices digitally with all residents.

### Parking Management
Organize resident and visitor parking efficiently with digital allocation and monitoring.

### Amenity Booking
Enable residents to reserve clubhouses, gyms, sports courts, meeting halls, and other shared facilities seamlessly.

### Admin Dashboard
Give management committees complete visibility into operations, requests, payments, and resident activity.
