# Local Advertising & Marketplace

## Empower Local Community Commerce

Create a thriving hyperlocal marketplace where residents and businesses can connect, promote, and trade within the community.

NeighbourHUB helps apartment societies support local commerce while giving residents easier access to trusted products, services, and community deals.

---

## Features

### Local Business Promotions
Allow nearby businesses to promote relevant products and services directly to residents.

### Resident Buy & Sell Marketplace
Enable residents to buy, sell, exchange, or donate items within the community.

### Home Needs & Daily Essentials
Promote groceries, home services, maintenance products, and everyday essentials conveniently.

### Sponsored Society Promotions
Run community-sponsored campaigns, festive offers, and society announcements.

### Community Recommendations
Help residents discover trusted businesses and services through local recommendations.

### Hyperlocal Visibility
Give small businesses direct access to highly targeted residential audiences.

---

## Why It Matters

- Supports local businesses and entrepreneurs
- Creates a trusted community commerce ecosystem
- Helps residents discover relevant nearby services
- Encourages sustainable reuse through resident marketplaces
- Strengthens neighbourhood connections through local interactions
