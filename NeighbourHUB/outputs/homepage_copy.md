# Smart Living for Modern Communities

Manage security, resident communication, maintenance, home services, and local commerce — all from one powerful community platform.

Built for apartments, gated communities, and urban neighbourhoods.

## Everything Your Community Needs, Connected in One Platform

NeighbourHUB brings residents, management committees, security teams, and local service providers together through a seamless digital experience.

From visitor approvals and maintenance tracking to resident engagement and trusted home services, NeighbourHUB simplifies daily community life while helping societies operate more efficiently.

Designed for modern apartment living, the platform makes communities safer, more connected, and easier to manage.

## Features Overview

### Security & Visitor Management
Smart entry management with instant visitor approvals, delivery tracking, gate passes, and emergency alerts.

### Community Engagement
Keep residents connected with society updates, group discussions, event announcements, polls, and celebrations.

### Home Services Marketplace
Discover trusted local professionals for cleaning, repairs, appliance servicing, tutoring, wellness, and more.

### Society Maintenance & Operations
Handle complaints, maintenance requests, payments, notices, parking, and amenities with ease.

### Local Marketplace & Advertising
Support local businesses and enable residents to buy, sell, and promote services within the community.
