# Home Services Marketplace

## Trusted Home Services at Your Fingertips

Give residents instant access to reliable local professionals for everyday household needs.

NeighbourHUB connects apartment communities with verified service providers, making home maintenance and personal services faster, safer, and more convenient.

---

## Features

### Maid & Housekeeping Services
Find trusted domestic help and regular housekeeping support within your community network.

### Electricians & Plumbers
Book skilled professionals for repairs, installations, and emergency fixes.

### Appliance Repair Services
Get quick assistance for home appliances including refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, and more.

### Cleaning Services
Schedule deep cleaning, sofa cleaning, bathroom cleaning, and move-in or move-out services.

### Tutors & Trainers
Discover local tutors, yoga instructors, fitness trainers, and wellness professionals.

### Easy Service Booking
Request services directly from the app with a smooth booking experience.
