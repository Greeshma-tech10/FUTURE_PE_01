# Community Engagement

## Build Stronger Neighbour Connections

Create a vibrant and interactive community experience where residents stay informed, engaged, and connected every day.

NeighbourHUB makes communication effortless by bringing announcements, discussions, events, and community interactions into one easy-to-use platform.

---

## Features

### Society Feed & Updates
Share important announcements, community news, and resident updates in real time.

### Group Chats & Discussions
Enable residents to connect, discuss topics, and collaborate within dedicated community groups.

### Events & Celebrations
Organize festival celebrations, society gatherings, workshops, and resident activities with ease.

### Polls & Resident Voting
Collect opinions, conduct polls, and make community decisions transparently.

### Lost & Found
Help residents quickly report and recover misplaced items within the community.

### Community Announcements
Keep everyone informed with instant notices and emergency communication.

---

## Why Communities Love It

- Encourages stronger neighbour relationships
- Improves communication across residents and management
- Increases participation in community activities
- Creates a more connected and active society experience
- Reduces information gaps and missed updates
